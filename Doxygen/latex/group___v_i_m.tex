\doxysubsection{Подробное описание}
Vectored Interrupt Manager. 

The vectored interrupt manager (VIM) provides hardware assistance for prioritizing and controlling the many interrupt sources present on a device. Interrupts are caused by events outside of the normal flow of program execution.
