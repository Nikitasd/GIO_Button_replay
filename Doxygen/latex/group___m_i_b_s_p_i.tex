\doxysubsection{Подробное описание}
Multi-\/\+Buffered Serial Peripheral Interface Module. 

The Mib\+SPI/\+Mib\+SPIP is a high-\/speed synchronous serial input/output port that allows a serial bit stream of programmed length (2 to 16 bits) to be shifted in and out of the device at a programmed bit-\/transfer rate. The Mib\+SPI has a programmable buffer memory that enables programmed transmission to be completed without CPU intervention
