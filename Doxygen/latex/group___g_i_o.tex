\doxysubsection{Подробное описание}
General-\/\+Purpose Input/\+Output Module. 

The GIO module provides the family of devices with input/output (I/O) capability. The I/O pins are bidirectional and bit-\/programmable. The GIO module also supports external interrupt capability.
