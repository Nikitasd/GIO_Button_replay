\doxysubsection{Подробное описание}
Serial Peripheral Interface Module. 

SPI is a high-\/speed synchronous serial input/output port that allows a serial bit stream of programmed length (2 to 16 bits) to be shifted in and out of the device at a programmed bit-\/transfer rate.
